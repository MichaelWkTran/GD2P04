Press 1 to disable all particle effects
Press 2 to enable CPU particles
Press 3 to enable GPU particles

Use the WASD keys to move the camera. Hold the SPACE key to move the camera up. Hold the LEFT CONTROL key to move the camera down. Hold the LEFT MOUSE BUTTON and move your mouse to rotate the camera. 

 - Billboarding is applied to CPU and GPU particles.
 - Deffered.exe has 5 lights: a white directional light, a red point light, a green point light, a blue point light, a yelow point light.
